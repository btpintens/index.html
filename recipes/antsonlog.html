Ants on a Log

The name of this dish isn't particularly appetizing and - frankly - neither is the dish. But, it is a fun way to trick the innocent into eating vegetables and getting a healthy dose of fiber.

Ingredients

  • celery stalks
  • peanut butter
  • raisins

Steps

  1. thoroughly wash and dry the celery
  2. divide each stalk into halves or thirds: each piece should be about the length of your thumb
  3. fill the celery cavity with peanut butter
  4. place raisins on top ala ants on a log
